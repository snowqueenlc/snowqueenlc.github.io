# Books & Resources

This page is for readers who want to explore physical copies of the Bible or the Torah. Many people prefer holding a book in their hands, and there are beautiful editions available in stores and online.

## Why I Include Physical Books

Some readers enjoy comparing translations, studying historical editions, or simply having a personal copy they can mark and revisit. Offering guidance here allows visitors to explore these texts in a way that feels grounded and personal.

## How to Find These Books

You can find Bibles and Torah books in many places, including:

- Local bookstores  
- Online retailers  
- Secondhand shops  
- Library sales  
- Faith-based bookstores  

You are welcome to search for the editions that speak to you. I will also be adding curated suggestions in the future.

## Charity Support

When I begin offering selected books through this site, any profits beyond basic costs will be donated to:

- **Compassion International**

This project is meant to support learning, dignity, and compassion.
